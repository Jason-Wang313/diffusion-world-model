# Limitations Notes

The evidence is controlled and toy-scale. The learned diffusion models are intentionally small and CPU-first. The environment is diagnostic rather than realistic.

Pilot-label repair is a scoped controlled result, not a universal guarantee. It relies on support-covered regimes where the observed generated-future features, uncertainty, consistency, or supplied upper-bound hazard features identify the selected-tail error. If two candidates are observationally identical but differ in hidden real utility, feature-only selection cannot always recover the oracle choice.

Larger benchmarks with independently measured real utility would be needed before making broad robotics claims.
